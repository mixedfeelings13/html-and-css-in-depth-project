ABOUT

Based in Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a boutique hair salon that specializes in cut, color, and styling. We also offer makeup and nail services, priding ourselves on our warm and relaxing atmosphere. Hair Day is best known for edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts. Please note that our salon can only accommodate a small number of coloring appointments each week, so we recommend booking far in advance.

To keep our clients inspired between appointments, our talented employees share makeup and nail tutorials on the Hair Day website.

Hair Day was founded by Pria, a certified colorist who immediately started specializing in edgy, custom coloring techniques. Pria, along with her good friend and talented hairdresser, Garry, opened the salon to create a "treat yourself" environment for their clients. Our staff consists of seven members, including two stylists, three colorists, one manicurist, and one makeup artist.
